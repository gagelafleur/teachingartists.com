About ATA

The Association of Teaching Artists (ATA) is a practitioner led national network and community of practice for Teaching Artists. The Association of Teaching Artists (ATA) was founded at a summit in Poughkeepsie, New York in April 1998. New York State Teaching Artists, arts administrators, and leaders in statewide arts funding came together at the request of The New York State Council on The Arts to consider the need and feasibility of forming an organization of Teaching Artists.
