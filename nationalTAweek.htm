National Teaching Artist Appreciation Week

In 2012 The Association of Teaching Artists declared the third week in May as National Teaching Artists Appreciation Week.  Teaching Artists are important and integral parts of quality education and vibrant communities, and the third week in May ATA officially celebrates their many contributions to making life better for so many children and adults.
