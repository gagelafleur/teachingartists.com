Jamie Topper
 www.jamietopper.com
jamie@jamietopper.com 		 
   
Jamie Topper has worked as a Teaching Artist in Chicago schools and community settings for over 12 years for nationally renowned arts organizations such as Chicago Arts Partnerships in Education (CAPE), Snow City Arts, Columbia College's Center for Community Arts Partnerships and Project AIM (Arts Integration Mentorship), Urban Gateways, After School Matters, Northeastern University's Chicago Teacher's Center, and Interfaith Refugee and Immigration Ministries.  She is a percussionist specializing in Afro-Caribbean and Middle Eastern rhythmic traditions.  In 2010 Topper was awarded the acclaimed 3Arts Teaching Artist Award for her work (http://3arts.org/artist/jamie-topper/).  She is currently pursuing a Masters degree in Public Humanities at Brown University.
