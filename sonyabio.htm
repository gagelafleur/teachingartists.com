Sonya Robinson
Co-Chair
 www.artistcorpsnola.org
soronola@gmail.com  		  
   
Sonya Robinson has over 20 years experience in the field of arts learning, creating programs for educational, nonprofit, and corporate audiences. An accomplished singer and actor, she has served as a teaching artist in over 50 schools, with students ranging from early childhood through graduate level programs. As part of the Higher Education Collaborative at Lincoln Center Institute, she partnered with colleges and universities to introduce pre-service teachers to the pedagogy of aesthetic education. She has designed and conducted professional development programs for teaching artists and educators throughout the United States, including the New York City Department of Education, Lincoln Center Institute, Symphony Space, City Lights Youth Theatre, Ballet Hispanico, the Tennessee Performing Arts Center, and Nashville Metropolitan Parks and Recreation. Since moving to New Orleans in 2008, she has served as an arts learning consultant for the Louisiana Division of the Arts, Mississippi Arts Commission, Baton Rouge Arts Council, Jefferson and Orleans Parish Schools, and KID smART. Sonya currently serves as Director of Artist Corps New Orleans, an organization that engages musicians in artist-driven service to local schools and the community, furthering the field of teaching artistry as it expands within the national service movement.
