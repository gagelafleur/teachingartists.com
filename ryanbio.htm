Ryan Hurley
 www.artsatlargeinc.org
 ryan@artsatlargeinc.org 		 
   

Ryan Hurley is an educator, writer, community organizer and currently the Program Manager for Arts @ Large, a Milwaukee-based arts education organization that provides comprehensive art and community-based programming for public schools and was the winner of the Americans for the Arts 2012 Arts Education Award. His experience working with youth across diverse learning environments from in-home therapy for children with autism to being a camp facilitator for children in transitional housing has helped shape his current work as an arts education administrator. Ryan is a member of four National Poetry Slam teams and has been featured in multiple national publications including The Progressive, Burning Word, Dream of a Nation and Positive Impact Magazine. Ryan has served on the Americans for the Arts Emerging Leaders Council and has a Master’s Degree in Cultural Foundations of Education.
